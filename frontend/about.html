About The Expert Academy

Founded by Sher Wali Dar, The Expert Academy has been providing quality education for 3 years, establishing itself as a premier educational institution in Pakistan. Located near the scenic Shalley Valley Range Road, we offer comprehensive education from Nursery through Class 12.

We are committed to academic excellence and providing students with a strong foundation in their chosen fields.
